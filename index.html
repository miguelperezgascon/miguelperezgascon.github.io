Equation Solver

Equation Solver — an advanced symbolic and numerical engine implemented in Python. It supports parsing expressions with the Shunting-Yard algorithm, building full ASTs, evaluating real and complex values, and applying symbolic differentiation. Equipped with multiple solvers — from polynomial methods (Companion matrix, Durand-Kerner) to Brent’s method — it can handle both exact and approximate solutions for linear, polynomial, and transcendental equations.
